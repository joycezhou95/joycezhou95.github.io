ART/DESIGN
I started developing my artistic skills through traditional mediums. At first I focused solely on graphite still life drawings. As I become more comfortable with graphite, I started painting, specifically watercolor. Afterwards I branched out to many different mediums, specializing specifically in graphite and ink.

In my AP 2D Design high school class, I started learning more digital mediums like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. I refined my Photoshop skills when I joined the high school robotics team as the graphic design lead. As the graphic designer, I had to create marketing fliers and banners to promote the team and the functions of the robot.

I have continued to create fliers in college and branched out to creating apparel for various teams. I have also started learning more about typography and creating typographic collages.
